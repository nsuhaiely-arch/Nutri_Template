Quick Fixes

The Smart Desk-Snack Selector

Tell us what you're feeling, and we'll recommend the optimal snack from your office pantry or vending machine.

The Energy Booster

Greek Yogurt with a handful of Berries. The protein-carb combo provides a steady release of glucose without the spike.

The Focus Fuel

Walnuts or Almonds. Rich in Omega-3s and Vitamin E to protect brain cells and keep you sharp during long meetings.

The Calm Crunch

Dark Chocolate (70%+) or Herbal Tea. Magnesium in cocoa helps regulate cortisol levels during tight deadlines.

The Satiety Hero

Hard-boiled Egg or Apple slices with Nut Butter. High fiber and protein to keep hunger at bay until dinner.
